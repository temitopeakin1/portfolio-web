About Me

I’m Temitope Akinmegha, a Senior Software Engineer with over six years of experience building and leading the development of production-grade web applications across the stack, primarily in fintech and product-driven environments.

My experience spans frontend and backend development, along with building and integrating large language model–driven systems. I work with technologies including React, Next.js, Angular, Remix, Node.js, NestJS, and TypeScript, with focus on designing and delivering scalable, maintainable systems end-to-end. This includes frontend architecture, backend services, API design, and ensuring seamless integration across the entire application lifecycle.

I have played key roles in building payment platforms, loan systems, AI coding assistant, and complex dashboards where reliability, performance, and clean architecture are critical. Beyond writing code, I take ownership of technical direction, structuring systems, defining patterns, and making decisions that align engineering efforts with product and business goals.

I’m comfortable leading initiatives from concept to deployment, collaborating with engineers, product managers, and stakeholders to translate requirements into well-structured, scalable solutions. I place strong emphasis on code quality, performance optimization, and building systems that are easy for teams to comprehend,scale, and maintain. What I bring is a balance of technical depth, system thinking, and execution speed, ensuring teams can move quickly without sacrificing long-term stability.

I’m currently seeking opportunities where I can take ownership of critical systems, contribute to technical strategy, and help teams build robust, scalable products while continuing to grow as an engineer and leader.
